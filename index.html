Sylvia Yanes Portfolio

I'm a former lawyer turned future librarian with a Fujifilm X-T20 mirrorless camera like the one in the photo, and this is my portfolio. With the exception of the photo of the camera and my photo, all the photos you see in this portfolio were taken with my Fujifilm X-T20 and one of my lenses. These lenses are the XF 18-55mm F2.8-4 R, XF 35mm F1.4 R, and XF 56mm F1.2 R. While I shoot in both RAW and JPEG, the photos in my portfolio are unedited JPEG files. Contact me if you would like your photo taken!
